Specifications

Ultra-high speed SSD

Maximize your play sessions with near instant load times for installed PS5™ games with read speeds of up to 5.5 GB/s.

Integrated I/O

The custom integration of the PS5 systems lets creators pull data from the SSD so quickly that they can design games impossible before.

Up to 120fps

Enjoy smooth and fluid high frame rate gameplay at up to 120fps for compatible games, with support on 4K displays.

Backwards compatibility

Play a back catalog of 99% of PS4™ games on your PS5 console both digitally and physically.

Tempest 3D AudioTech

Immerse yourself in soundscapes where it feels as if the sound comes from every direction.

HDR technology

With an HDR TV, supported PS5 games display an unbelievably vibrant and lifelike range of colors.
